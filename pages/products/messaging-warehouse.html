Messaging Warehouse

SwiftNexus Messaging Warehouse gives you a secure, scalable way to store, access, and analyse high volumes of financial messaging data. It streamlines investigations, simplifies compliance, and brings real-time transparency to your operations.

  • Secure, scalable solution built for financial institutions
  • Store, retrieve, and analyse messaging data across systems
  • Improve efficiency, reduce manual workload, and drive smarter decisions

Control your financial messaging data—without the complexity.

Financial institutions face growing pressure to access, store, and analyse vast volumes of payment data instantly, securely, and in full regulatory alignment. Messaging Warehouse centralises your financial messaging by streamlining your reporting and simplifying audit readiness across all formats and channels.

Product Overview

Messaging Warehouse gives you instant access to both live and archived data, giving you complete visibility and control over all your financial data, with easy monitoring and alerting for live activities. With dynamic API integrations to banking channels, you can monitor traffic in real-time and trigger alerts for specific message types or content patterns. That means simpler compliance management, thanks to centralised message data and comprehensive audit trails.

Messaging Warehouse integrates seamlessly with your existing financial messaging systems and compliance tools, meaning there's minimal disruption to your workflow. And with the ability to generate routine or on-demand reports and dashboards, you'll have powerful insights at your fingertips, enabling you to make more informed decisions.

Regulatory Compliance & Standards

Messaging Warehouse aligns with critical financial and data protection regulations including ISO 20022, AMLD, PSD2, GDPR, CSP and FATF, ensuring compliance across all messaging storage and retrieval functions.

The system automatically maintains comprehensive audit trails for all messaging activities, supporting compliance reporting while reducing manual documentation efforts. By automating routine compliance processes, Messaging Warehouse significantly reduces the operational burden associated with regulatory adherence. It enables teams to spend less time and effort on compliance and documentation, instead focusing their efforts on high-value tasks, improving overall efficiency while keeping costs down.
